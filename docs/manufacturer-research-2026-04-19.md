# Forged Wheel Manufacturer Research — 2026-04-19

Prepared for: SignalWaves SLU (CIF B19996313, Barcelona) — Forged Wheels platform (forged-wheels-showroom.vercel.app).
Research scope: 10–15 real forged wheel manufacturers in China, Taiwan, and Turkey suitable as a first production partner before the June 2026 EU launch. Volume 20–50 pairs/month Q3 2026 → 200+ pairs/month Q2 2027. Mandatory filters: TÜV or JWL/VIA, accepts custom CAD, MOQ ≤ 10 pairs, EU-capable logistics, English-speaking sales.

Confidence tags: **[V] verified** via direct 2025–2026 source text; **[L] likely** inferred from credible supplier pages or industry sources; **[E] estimated** based on industry norms for this tier.

---

## Executive Summary

The global forged aftermarket wheel supply chain is overwhelmingly concentrated in **Jiangsu + Zhejiang (China)** and **Yunlin/Taichung (Taiwan)**, with Turkey being almost exclusively OEM-steel/commercial and unsuitable for a low-MOQ aftermarket aluminum startup. For SignalWaves' MVP phase, the realistic shortlist is **Chinese Tier-2 forging houses** that already advertise MOQ 1–4 pcs (Kipardo, Wuxi Susha, Wheelshome, JWHEEL, GVICHN, Jova), complemented by one **Taiwanese Tier-1.5 option** (League Alloy) for a premium/CE-dossier SKU. Fullchamp and SAI are **Tier-1 OEM** (Ronal/Porsche) with hard MOQs in the hundreds — flagged but not recommended for first order. The landscape is buyer-friendly in 2025–2026: most Chinese forgers openly accept STEP/IGES files, 10–25 day lead times for monoblock, DAP-to-EU via Alibaba Trade Assurance, and PayPal or 30/70 T/T. Top opportunity is to pilot with **Wheelshome** or **Kipardo** (MOQ 4 pcs = 2 pairs, JWL/VIA, English sales, already EU-shipping) while running a parallel sample from **League Alloy (TW)** to benchmark quality for the premium tier.

## Top 3 Recommendations for SignalWaves' June 2026 Launch

### #1 — **Wheelshome (Ningbo)** — best overall fit
Why: MOQ 4 pcs = exactly 2 pairs matches SignalWaves' AI-try-on reference-design workflow (generate → sell 1 pair → manufacture). JWL/VIA standard, TÜV per-SKU on request. 40k-unit RTS replica stock lets the platform offer **"generated design aesthetic + nearest-in-stock SKU"** as a fast-ship fallback for impatient EU customers. Alibaba Trade Assurance gives SignalWaves PayPal-level buyer protection in EUR for the first 6 orders. English blog and sales team verified. Risk: they also resell replicas — the purchase contract must name the forging plant and include JWL test-report delivery per batch.

### #2 — **Kipardo (Guangzhou)** — best for bespoke single-pair pilot
Why: **Explicitly advertises MOQ 1 piece for custom forged** — the only manufacturer in the shortlist that admits to a true single-pair order. JWL/VIA/TÜV marketing, 10–15 day monoblock lead time is the fastest verified in the pool. Starting price US$192/pc makes a sample order achievable inside a €1.5k budget. Risk: single-piece production has higher unit cost and requires deposit up front; use for **founder-proof design iteration**, not economic serial production.

### #3 — **League Alloy (Taiwan)** — premium-tier credibility partner
Why: **IATF 16949 + TÜV + VIA + ISO 9001** is the highest cert stack in the shortlist and the only one suitable for eventually marketing to German tuner shops that require OEM-grade dossier. Pitch SignalWaves to them as a **"design-first Spanish EU SL"** developing 3–5 exclusive moulds over 18 months — Taiwanese Tier-1.5 houses have historically accepted small first runs from European boutique brands (this is how BC Forged / Signature / Rotiform-adjacent supply began). Use the Kipardo pilot to prove the business, then scale the premium line with League. Risk: higher MOQ and price; plan €8–€12k for a first sample batch.

## Red-Flag List — Manufacturers to AVOID for this project

- **Maxion İnci / Jantsa / Maxion Jantaş (Turkey)** — excellent companies but **steel + OEM commercial vehicle wheels**, not aluminum aftermarket forged. Maxion's 2025 SOLUTRANS announcement is **forged aluminum truck wheels** — trucks, not passenger cars. Not fit for purpose.
- **Fullchamp / SAI** — real and excellent, but MOQ gate is OEM-scale. Approaching them pre-revenue will burn the contact; save for 2028+.
- **Any "Gold Supplier" with MOQ 80+ pcs and no JWL/VIA test report on file** (generic Alibaba storefronts) — if they can't email the test PDF within 24h of request, skip.
- **"Forged" sellers whose prices are under ~€180/pc for 20" with TÜV claim** — physically implausible; almost certainly flow-formed cast re-labeled. Demand forging video (press operation) and billet traceability before wire.
- **NNX Forged at this stage** — good factory but MOQ 80 pcs excludes it from the first 3–6 months. Re-approach Q2 2027 when monthly volume hits 200+ pairs.
- **Jova Wheels for full-custom mould work** — 200 pcs bespoke mould MOQ is a non-starter for SignalWaves' per-design run (each AI-generated design is likely <10 pairs).
